How It Works

Six clear steps from
idea to approved permit

A streamlined process with set deliverables at every stage — you always know exactly where your project stands.

Step 1 · Consultation

Request a Free Quote

We start with a conversation about your project, your property, and your jurisdiction's requirements. You get a flat-fee quote and a clear timeline — no hourly surprises, no vague scopes.

Get a Quote Today
Step 2 · Sheet A100

Site & Existing Conditions

We pull survey data and assessor records, and field-verify existing conditions for remodels and additions — establishing the accurate ground truth your design is built on, so nothing gets redrawn later.

Step 3 · Schematic

Schematic Design

Floor plans and elevations take shape with your input, with revision rounds built into the scope. Whether simple or complex, every design is tailored to your needs, your lot, and your zoning.

Step 4 · Visualization

3D Model & Renderings

Your design becomes a complete 3D model with photoreal renderings, inside and out. You approve the real home — the light, the spaces, the finishes — while changes are still easy and free.

Step 5 · Sheet A103

Construction Documents

We produce the complete coordinated set — architectural through structural — drawn to 2024 IBC, IRC, and IECC with your city's amendments, and formatted to its submittal checklist.

Step 6 · Sheet A201

Permit Approval & Handoff

We submit to the city, track plan review, and answer every redline until your permit is approved. Your contractor receives a build-ready set, and we stay available through construction.
