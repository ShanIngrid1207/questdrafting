The Studio

Eighteen years turning structural engineering into drawings crews can actually build from.

Quest is a Scottsdale drafting house working across Arizona and the Southwest. We produce structural drawings, LOD 350 Revit models and permit sets for general contractors, developers, fabricators and the engineers who stamp them.

No offshore handoffs, no template sets. Every project is drawn by a detailer who reads the calcs, adopts your standards, and owns it through plan check.

How we work

From your scope to a permit-ready set.

Five steps, no mystery — a clear, predictable path from the first sketch to plan-check approval.

Step 01

Share your scope

Send sketches, an architect's set or markups. We return a fixed quote within one business day.

Step 02

We draft & model

A dedicated detailer produces drawings and BIM models in your standards and conventions.

Step 03

Coordinate

We run the model against the other trades, resolve conflicts, and flag what the field would catch.

Step 04

Engineer review

Calculations are checked and drawings stamped by a licensed PE where required.

Step 05

Permit-ready delivery

You receive a clean, coordinated set — and we answer plan-check comments until approved.
