§ 06 / How we work

A clear, predictable path from your scope to a permit-ready set — five steps, no mystery.

01

Share your scope

Send sketches, an architect's set or markups. We review and return a fixed quote within one business day.

02

We draft & model

A dedicated detailer produces drawings and BIM models in your standards, title blocks and naming conventions.

03

Coordinate & clash-check

We run the model against the other trades, resolve conflicts, and flag anything the field would have caught the hard way.

04

Engineer review

Calculations are checked and drawings stamped by a licensed PE where the jurisdiction requires it.

05

Permit-ready delivery

You receive a clean, coordinated set — and we answer plan-check comments until it's approved.
