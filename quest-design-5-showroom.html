The Studio

Eighteen years turning structural engineering into drawings crews can actually build from.

Quest is a Scottsdale drafting house working across Arizona and the Southwest. We produce structural drawings, LOD 350 Revit models and permit sets for general contractors, developers, fabricators and the engineers who stamp them.

No offshore handoffs, no template sets. Every project is drawn by a detailer who reads the calcs, adopts your standards, and owns it through plan check.
